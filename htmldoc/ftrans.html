ftrans

PURPOSE

FTRANS Transform force/moment

SYNOPSIS

function Ft = ftrans(T, F)

DESCRIPTION 

FTRANS Transform force/moment

    FT = FTRANS(T, F)

 Transforms a force/moment F in the base frame to FT in the frame T.
 F and FT are 6-vectors of the form [Fx Fy Fz Mx My Mz]

 SEE ALSO: DIFF2TR
